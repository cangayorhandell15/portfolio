ABOUT

I-TECH is an Ar Games, it was created on october to november 2023 by 8 people. it was made from scratch and lack of knowledge but we strive harder to accomplish it, to learn what we need to learn to finish what we started. This game is for everyone, if you are bored or your are down just play our game and our rewards can give you motivation to stand again. You can play it wherever you want, enjoy our game and take your deserving reward and we know that reward will help you on your life.

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Download our game. Download Game
  2. Download the QR Code and instruction to play the game. Download QR and Instruction
  3. Always scan ground plane and wait for plane indicator to appear, before you scan qr code.
